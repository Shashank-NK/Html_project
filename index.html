What is a Machine?

A machine is a device designed to make work easier by transforming energy into useful motion or action. From simple tools like levers and pulleys to complex systems such as computers and engines, machines play a vital role in daily life. They increase efficiency, accuracy, and speed, allowing humans to accomplish tasks that would otherwise require great effort or time. Modern machines often combine mechanical, electrical, and digital components, enabling automation and intelligent decision-making. As technology advances, machines continue to reshape industries, improve living standards, and influence how people work, communicate, and solve problems in an increasingly connected world.
